How to buy $TROLLCOIN

Get a Wallet: Set up a wallet compatible with the Soalana blockchain (like Phantom).

Add Soalana (SOL): Deposit SOL into your wallet.

Visit Raydium: Go to Raydium and connect your wallet.

Select Tokens: Choose SOL as "From" and paste the $TROLLCOIN contract address as "To."

Swap: Enter the amount of SOL you want to swap for $TROLLCOIN and confirm the transaction.

Confirm and Wait: Confirm the swap in your wallet and wait for it to process.
